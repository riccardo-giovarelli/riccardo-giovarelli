

## :wave: Hi there!

My name is Riccardo Giovarelli.

I've loved Computer Science ever since I first discovered it.

I wrote my first software when I was twelve with MS-DOS QBasic, the only programming language I could use, as it was the only one installed on my first PC (a PC without an Internet connection!).

Now I have a bachelor's degree in Computer Science and I'm a professional Full Stack developer but, every time I open my laptop, I feel like the same 12 year old boy who turned on his first PC years ago. I have the same enthusiasm and curiosity, just more experience.
