

## Hi there!

My name is Riccardo Giovarelli.

I love Computer Science since I discovered its existence.

I wrote my first software when I was twelve with MS-DOS QBasic, the only programming language I could use, because it was the only one installed in my first PC and my PC didn't have Internet connection at the time.

Now I'm a professional Full Stack developer but, every time I open my laptop, I'm the same 12 years old that turn on his first pc. Same enthusiasm and curiosity, just with more experience.
